Ever wish your Slack had a bot that could answer questions and remember what you talked about?

AI Slack Bot is a straightforward, plug-and-play bot that brings conversational AI to a single Slack channel. After a simple setup, you can create a channel for the bot and start chatting.

By default, its answers are clear and to the point, but you can update its system prompt to give it a different personality if you like.

It remembers context in its channel, so you can ask follow-up questions and keep the conversation going.
Straightforward Linux Ubuntu setup, a base line you can add to:
- Works in the one channel you configure during Slack app setup
- Powered by modern AI (Gemini, OpenAI, and more)
- Replies in threads to keep your channel tidy
- Remembers what you talked about in its channel
